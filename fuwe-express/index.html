About Us


FUWE EXPRESS is a privately owned Malawian transportation and general dealing company, founded in January 2019 by a Malawian dedicated to offering quality weAre and products nationwide.
As a company, FUWE EXPRESS believes that ethical behavior creates good will and reputation that expands opportunities. Since inception, FUWE EXPRESS endeavors to gain and maintain goodwill and very favorable relationships with potential clients.

Vision Statement

Our Vision is to provide quality products and services as well as a competent workforce with passion, humor and skills to contribute to the betterment of our client’s growth.

We strive to make employees a key priority and place clients at the center of our operations and organize ourselves to meet and exceed their needs and expectations. Striving to meet the twin objectives of high quality and low cost by utilizing modern technologies in production, goaling and supply.

Mission Statement

To provide clientele focused, relevant products and services in Malawi, efficiently and effectively, leaving all stakeholders satisfied.



Goals and Objectives

  • To accomplish the company’s mission.

  • To provide full time employment to more than 100 Malawians in the next two(2) years and be one of the goal leaders in the next five(5) years of its operation.
